StressSync Project

Project Overview

StressSync is an innovative application designed to help students manage their time and stress effectively. It integrates directly with Apple Watches to provide real-time feedback and suggestions based on monitoring heart rate and activity levels.

More details can be found on the project's dedicated website: Time for a Solution.
